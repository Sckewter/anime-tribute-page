The journey of Edward Elric:

  • Height -4'11"
  • Limbs - His arm and leg were lost when he was young so he now has metallic limbs called,"automail" that he can change the shape of using alchemy
  • Brother - His brother is Alphonse Elric who is bound to a body of armor for many years losing his actual body in an alchemist accident when they were both younger
  • Hates - One thing Edward couldn't stand was cow's milk and some say that is why he is so short
  • Alchemy - Edward and his brother are experts at alchemy learning it when they were very young
  • Military - When Edward lost his limbs from the accident the military offered a spot with pay in exchange for his skills so he and his brother joined to fight for a cause becoming a state alchemist
  • Parents - His Mom passed away when he was very young and his Dad left only coming back on occasion
  • Style - Edward's alchemy allows him to excel in hand to hand combat transforming his automail into a blade which can slice and dice enemies with ease
  • Later life - When the brother succeed in attaining Alphonse's body back Edward loses his ability to use alchemy however he still enjoyed day to day life after
  • Fun facts - As an avid traveler, Edward codes his own alchemy research notes in the guise of a travelogue. They are so well coded that not even his brother Alphonse can decipher them.
  • Fun facts - Edward's name means "rich guard", derived from the Old English elements ead "wealth, fortune" and weard "guard".
